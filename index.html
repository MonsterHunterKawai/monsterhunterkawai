Is $KAWAI a token or a NFT?

$KAWAI is a token and NFT hybrid. The "ERC404" token standard, a popular similar token standard, allows for deep liquidity with NFTs while transacting with either the token or the NFT itself

You'll be able to see your KAWAI cats on DAPP or on Opensea. Make sure you equip armor your KAWAI cats on DAPP.
